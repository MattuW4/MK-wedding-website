There are also plenty of hotels and B&B’s for guests in the surrounding area, details of which can be found below.

Camping and Caravans

For guests who’d like to stay on-site, there is space for camping under the stars. Facilities include hot showers, toilets, a dedicated camping kitchen, and a room for getting ready. Guests are welcome to arrive at the venue early to set up tents and get settled before the celebrations begin.

If you’re planning to camp or would like more information, please let us know, and we’ll be happy to send you more details to help with your plans.

Whether you’re bringing a tent, a campervan, or a glamping setup, we want to make sure your stay is as comfortable as possible.

Where To Stay?

There are also plenty of hotels and B&B’s for guests in the surrounding area, details of which can be found below.

Camping and Caravans

The Camping Field in front of the house is perfect for guests who enjoy sleeping under canvas or in campervans or caravans. There’s a campfire and barbecue area, as well as access to a camping kitchen and shower in an old stable block. If you’d prefer to glamp, we can arrange fully furnished bell tents ready for your arrival.

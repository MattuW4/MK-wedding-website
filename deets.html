Details, details, details

Here is some information about the wedding celebrations. More will be provided as the day gets closer. Don’t hesitate to contact us if you need any additional information or have a question.

Venue Info

Where is the venue and what do I need to know?

We’re delighted to be getting married at Bromwich Park, a beautiful venue set in the countryside. Both our ceremony and reception will be held at the venue, so there’s no need to travel between locations.

For those who would like to stay overnight, there is space at the venue for camping under the stars. Check the camping information for more details on the setup.

RSVP

When should I RSVP by?

We can’t wait to celebrate with you! Please let us know by the 1st of June 2026 if you’ll be able to join us.

If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to let us know.

Invite list

Can I bring a Plus One?

If your plus one is included on the guest list, their name will be listed on the RSVP.

Unfortunately, we are only able to accommodate those formally invited on your wedding invitation. We truly appreciate your understanding!

Dress Code

What can I wear?

We love a bit of fabulousness, so we’re asking our guests to outshine the bride! - think vibrant, playful, and festive!

If you have an outfit at the back of your wardrobe you’ve always wanted to wear, but thought was too much, now’s your moment.

Travel & Parking

What is the situation?

There is ample on-site parking available for all guests, and the parking area will be clearly signposted along the lane. Vehicles may be left overnight, including for those who are camping. We kindly ask that all cars are collected the following day. If you need accessible parking please let us know - we’re happy to help.

Check back as more information will be shared here shortly on transportation...

The Big day timeline

What can I expect on the big day?

Guests should arrive between 1:30pm and 1:45pm, with our ceremony starting around 2:00pm. After the ceremony, please join us for welcome drinks and canapés while we take a few photos.

Later in the afternoon, we’ll enjoy a family-style meal, followed by music, games, and plenty of opportunities to celebrate together. Late-night food will also be served.

For guests camping, you’re encouraged to arrive earlier to set up and get settled before the celebrations begin.

Closer to the big day, we’ll share additional details to help with your plans.

Photography & Videography

Can I take pictures & videos during the day?

We’re so excited to make memories with you on our special day! We’ll have an official photographer capturing the big moments, and you’re welcome to snap pictures. For the ceremony itself, we kindly ask that phones and other devices be put away so we can all be present and enjoy the moment together - thank you for understanding.

There will be plenty of opportunities throughout the day for fun photos with beautiful backdrops, props, and other playful touches!

Babies & Children

Are children invited to the Ceremony & Reception?

We are so excited to celebrate this special occasion with you! If your child is included on the guest list, their name will be listed on the RSVP. Due to venue regulations we are limited on the number of little ones who can join us on the day. All babies under 1yo, carriable in arms are welcome to both ceremony and reception. If you are unsure then please get in touch to answer any questions.

Food & Drink

What will the food & drink options be?

The Meal

Our meal will be served family-style and the food will have a Mediterranean inspiration. Don’t worry, omnivores — even though we’re both vegetarians, there will be plenty of options for everyone, including vegan choices. Our fabulous caterer will also accommodate dietary requirements, which we’ll ask about when you RSVP. If you have a strict allergy or special dietary need, please get in touch directly.

Drinks

Complimentary drinks will be provided at key moments during the day, but there will also be a bar where you can order additional drinks. Card payments are preferred, though cash is also accepted.

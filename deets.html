Photography & Videography

Can I take pictures & videos during the day?

We love that you want to make memories with us on this special day that might include pictures and videos. We will have an official photographer during the big moments and are happy for you to take a snap of a special scene but we would please ask that phones and other devices are put away for the ceremony itself so that we can all be present and enjoy the occasion together - thanks for understanding (don't worry, there will be plenty of photo ops that include fabulous backdrops, props and other fun throughout the day).

Babies & Children

Are children invited to the Ceremony & Reception?

We are so excited to celebrate this special occasion with you! If your child is included on the guest list, their name will be listed on the RSVP. Due to venue regulations we are limited on the number of little ones who can join us on the day. All babies under 1yo, carriable in arms are welcome to both ceremony and reception. If you are unsure then please get in touch to answer any questions.

Food & Drink

What will the food & drink options be?

Family style with a Mediterranean inspiration. Omnivores, worry not; we will be catering for all despite being veggies ourselves so there will be plenty to eat & plenty to choose from (including vegan options). We have a fab cater who will be catering for dietary requirements which we will ask for when you RSVP. If you have a strict allergy or dietary restriction please get in touch.

We will have complimentary drinks provided for you to enjoy at key moments during the day but there will also be a bar where you can order drinks from. They prefer card payments but will also accept cash.
